Frappe Books is a modern desktop accounting application built for small and medium businesses. It runs fully offline on Windows, macOS, and Linux, stores all data locally in SQLite, and covers everything from double-entry bookkeeping to Point of Sale operations.

## Get started quickly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Frappe Books">
    Download the installer for your platform from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/frappe/books/releases) or install via Homebrew on macOS/Linux.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the Setup Wizard">
    On first launch, the Setup Wizard guides you through creating your company, setting the fiscal year, and choosing your chart of accounts template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your accounts and items">
    Customize the Chart of Accounts to match your business, then add the items and services you sell or purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start recording transactions">
    Create your first sales invoice, record a payment, and check the General Ledger to see double-entry accounting in action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Frappe Books is 100% offline-first. All your financial data is stored in a local SQLite file on your machine — no internet connection required for day-to-day use.
</Note>
